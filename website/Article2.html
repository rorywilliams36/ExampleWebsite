Ever increasingly popular Website is exploring new games to add

The website The Puzzle Room has been looking at options to expand its arsenal of games on thier website. They currently have just the one which is very simple. The game requires the player to pick the object on the screen which is indicated on the screen. The game consists of three rounds where three images appear on the screen and the player has to pick the correct image.


The website has been exploring the ideas of adding other simple games such as 'Snap' and 'Snakes and Ladders'. The founder, Rory Williams says 'The website is mainly targetted at children in nursery who are just grasping the english language. This has casued us to look at simple game sto teach them the basics'.
